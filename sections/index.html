All the six (06) sections work on grammatical rules, phonetic rules, common mistakes etc. that the heads of sections have to prepare as lessons and share (not to say teach) with the members. And when a new member comes he/she is asked to choose the section he/she is interested in, because when you love something, doing it will be easier for you; and then it will be very easy to learn English through that hobby. All the sections generally work with their respective members on Mondays and Thursdays from 3p.m. to 6p.m. Finally, all the sections of the Club gather on Tuesdays from 3pm to 6pm for the General Meeting.
